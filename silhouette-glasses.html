Understanding the Essentials: Valid Prescription and PD (**Pupillary distance measurement for glasses**)

The Crucial Role of Pupillary Distance (PD)

Your pupillary distance measurement for glasses (PD) is the exact distance in millimeters between the centers of your pupils. This measurement is absolutely vital for ensuring your lenses are correctly aligned with your eyes. As Dr. Neil Pence, a respected figure from Indiana University School of Optometry, emphasizes, proper PD is paramount for clear vision, especially when dealing with stronger prescriptions. An incorrect PD can lead to eyestrain, headaches, and distorted vision, even with a perfectly accurate lens power.

One of the challenges consumers face is obtaining their PD. While some prescriptions might include it, many do not. Furthermore, some "big box" opticians may refuse to provide your PD if you choose not to purchase your glasses from their store, creating a hurdle for those looking to buy online prescription glasses.
