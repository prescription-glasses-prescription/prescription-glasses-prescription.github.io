Anti-Glare Prescription Lenses and Anti-Scratch Coatings

Reflections on your lenses can be incredibly distracting, reducing visual clarity and causing eye strain, especially when driving at night or working under artificial light. This is where anti-glare prescription lenses, also known as anti-reflective (AR) coatings, become invaluable. These coatings eliminate reflections from both the front and back surfaces of your lenses, allowing more light to pass through, which results in sharper vision, reduced eye fatigue, and improved aesthetics by making your eyes more visible to others.

Complementing anti-glare, anti-scratch coatings are equally vital for maintaining the longevity and clarity of your designer prescription glasses men's. While no lens is 100% scratch-proof, these hard coatings significantly increase resistance to daily wear and tear, extending the life of your eyewear and ensuring your vision remains consistently clear. They are a must-have for durability.
